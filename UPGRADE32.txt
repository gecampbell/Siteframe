UPGRADING FROM 3.1.x to 3.2.0

1. BACKUP YOUR FILES AND DATA!

2. Read #1, above.

3. Upgrade your database using sql/upgrade31to32.sql

4. Install the 3.2.0 files

If there are problems, restore from one of the backups you made in
steps 1 and 2 above.
